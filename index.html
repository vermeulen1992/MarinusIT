Who is this developer? | a brief history

I started with development after intermediate vocational education as a system administrator. For my expectation, there was no challenge in being a system administrator and decided to start a new study Software development.

Together with a friend we started and worked our way through college. With literally no programming knowledge or what so ever we needed to start quickly building our own programmes, games and mobile apps. A lot of students and even we were struggling with the high requirements of this study. It was bye bye with your social life and welcome to all the study books.

In the third year our graduation internship period stared at a Company Traxion B.V. who is specialized in Information security and Identity and Access Management. Still with that friend we have built a product called the Policy Discovery Tool what rights and policies from various systems imports. For example a Firewall or Active Directory is imported and compares these settings with earlier imports. The idea is to question yourself with why are these settings changed and by how?

The Policy Discovery Tool was a success and we graduated our study within 3.5 years. Higher professional education was done and we both got a job as software developer by Traxion Developers Services.

And the story continues...
